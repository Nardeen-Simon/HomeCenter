OLD TREE, CANTERBURY


An impressive four bedroom detached property in a traditional Kentish style on a select development. Finished to a high standard. The accommodation comprises: entrance hall, double reception room, kitchen/breakfast room open planned, utility room, four double bedrooms two of which have en suite shower rooms and in addition to this there is a family bathroom. Outside there is a very spacious double garage in the adjacent barn and further single garage also there and gardens which are mainly laid to lawn overlooking the central feature duck pond. There is a large gravelled driveway allowing ample off road parking.Just build Oak Lodge of Old Tree Farm is a development of three detached farm building style homes all set around central duck pond and surrounded by fields. Each property combines modern living with character style and tastefully decorated to a high standard.Currently tenanted.
